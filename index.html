About Us

We at Mice Make Soups believe that everyone deserves good soup to fill their bellies, no matter how small. Founded in 2024 by partners Theodore Whitefoot and Maisie Meadow, Mice Make Soups has expanded beyond a humble family business and is now a national movement. With over a dozen locations across the country, we are committed to providing delicious, wholesome soup to our community.

Our Soups

At Mice Make Soups, we offer a variety of soups to satisfy every palate. Our menu includes classic favorites like Chicken Noodle and Tomato Basil, as well as unique creations like our signature "Cheese and Cracker" soup. We use only the freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, to ensure that every bowl of soup is bursting with flavor.
